Research
======
My higher research goal focuses on AI-driven networks to enhance performance and security. I'm perticualrly intrested in in-network computing for telemetry and defense, operating systems, high-performance packet processing, and hardware-software co-design for intelligent networking systems. I am also passionate  about integrating AI into networked infrastructure. So far, my research has been published in top-tier venues, including USENIX NSDI, Security, and NDSS.
