I'm a CS Ph.D. candidate at [Wayne State University](https://wayne.edu) working under Dr. [Rhonhgo Jang](https://rhongho.github.io/). Prior to starting my Ph.D. I earned my Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Computer Engineering from the University of Tehran. I'm passionate about computer networks, system design, and understanding how complex systems work!

Research
======
My higher research goal focuses on AI-driven networks to enhance performance and security. I'm particularly intrested in in-network computing for telemetry and defense, operating systems, high-performance packet processing, and hardware-software co-design for intelligent networking systems. I am also passionate about integrating AI into networked infrastructure. So far, my research has been published in top-tier venues, including NSDI, NDSS, and Security.
